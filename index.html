Welcome to the Memory Game

How to Play:

1. Choose the number of players: 1 or 2.

2. Enter player names (for 2 players).

3. Choose numbers of pairs.

4. Click "Start Game" to begin.

5. Click on cards to reveal their numbers.

6. Match pairs of cards with the same number.

7. The player with the most matches wins!
